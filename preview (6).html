The Problem

Risk builds before anyone names it.

Task status isn't risk visibility. By the time teams see the problem, the cost of recovery has already started.

Scope creep grows quietly

Small additions accumulate weekly. Each looks manageable. Together they erode margin and compress timelines , invisibly.

Feedback arrives too late

Nine days in client review looks like two days in every project tool. Teams find out when it's already a timeline problem.

Reporting hides the real risk

Status reports reflect whoever compiled them, not the actual project risk state. Expensive to produce. Unreliable to rely on.

FAQ

Questions delivery teams ask first.

FLOWBASE is designed to support the tools teams already use, not create another place to manage tasks.

Does FLOWBASE replace our project management tool?
No. FLOWBASE is designed to work with the project data your team already uses. It reads delivery signals from trackers, spreadsheets and status reports, then turns them into risk visibility.
What data do we upload?
You can start with structured project data such as project names, owners, deadlines, task status, blocked work, feedback delays, scope changes and last update dates.
How does AI support the risk analysis?
The scoring engine detects risk signals. The AI layer explains why the risk is building and suggests the next action, such as assigning missing owners, confirming feedback deadlines or reviewing scope impact.
Is our project data secure?
The early audit is read only and uses only the information you choose to submit. FLOWBASE is being designed to analyze delivery signals without replacing or changing your existing project tools.
